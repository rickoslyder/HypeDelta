**RLHF (42 claims, 5%)**: Binary preference choices lose critical information about preference strength. **Robotics (39 claims, 4%)**: Tesla FSD V14.2 reportedly completing 2,732-mile coast-to-coast journey with zero interventions—framed as passing the "Physical Turing Test."
